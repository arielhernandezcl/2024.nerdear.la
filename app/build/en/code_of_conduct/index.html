Code of Conduct

GENERAL INFORMATION

Through this code of conduct, we establish the framework for the fundamentals of Nerdearla.
The main objective of the event is to create a space for collaborative exchange where we can have fun, learn, and share problems and knowledge.

WE TRUST DIVERSITY AS THE ETHICAL PRINCIPLE FUNDAMENTAL TO ANY EXCHANGE

We join efforts to redraw the paths of discrimination, exclusion, and condemnation of minorities who have suffered and still face greater difficulties to exercise their full competencies, capacities, and potentials with freedom and equal opportunities.

OUR SPIRIT IS TO PROMOTE A DISCRIMINATION-FREE ENVIRONMENT

We are aware of the multiple intersections that interfere with achieving this ultimate goal, so we believe it is our active duty, and that of the entire community, to engage in this challenge.

We expect sponsors, speakers, organizers, and all event attendees to respect the following principles of participation and collaboration:

  1. Maintain at all times a friendly and respectful dialogue that promotes the best version of the exchange possible.
  2. Respect diversity in all its forms: cultural, gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, age, professional, religious, ideological perspectives, etc.
  3. Do not discriminate, exclude, harass, or engage in acts that in any way undermine the dignity of individuals.
  4. Take responsibility for the shared space by taking care of it.
  5. Refrain from presenting material, images, or activities whose content may harm, diminish, or objectify individuals or groups.

CONTACT THE ORGANIZATION

Si observas y/o experimentas que estos principios no están siendo respetados, contacta con la organización vía WhatsApp al +54 9 11 2342-0868 y brindaremos la atención necesaria.

In the face of harassment, discrimination, or aggression behaviors, they are expected to cease immediately. Depending on the case, measures will be taken, which may include sanctions, expulsion, and/or reviewing future attendance to community events.

We expect to have the active cooperation of each participant to create an enriching and virtuous space that supports these policies and makes Nerdearla a place of heterogeneous, multiple, friendly, and inclusive encounters.
